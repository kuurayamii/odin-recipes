Pizza

Do you want to make a thin crust pizza of your own? Then you came to the perfect place to do so.

This recipe is about making the pizza dough giving you the free will of topping it with whatever you like!

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 tsp white sugar
  • 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Pre heat your oven at 260째C (Or 500째F)
  3. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 44째C (Or 110째F) in a small bowl. Let stand until the yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 8 minutes.
  4. Combine 1 3/4 cups flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in yeast mixture; mix well until dough comes together.
  5. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add remaining 1/4 cup flour if dough is too sticky
  6. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a greased pizza pan.
  7. Bake your dough for one minute and then pull it out the oven.
  8. Add your favourite toppings and bake it again for 5 more minutes or until it's golden brown.
